A bit more about me

I've been building products for over 12 years while serving in a design (and sometimes product management) capacity spanning enterprise SaaS, infrastructure technology, advertising, and consumer domains. I have a passion for how design is leveraged to drive innovation (tactically & strategically). Many of my past experiences have involved bringing a product from its very early inception, releasing it to market, and then continuing to refine it over time.

Most recently at WHOOP, I helped build and scale the function of design and product management to deliver innovative, customer-centric experiences that drove major business outcomes in D2C and B2B2C contexts.

I have a knack for being detail oriented and its where I naturally invest attention. I believe that good design is framed by good decision making. Good decision making is built upon having a strong understanding of the human, technical and business implications of a problem. Design cannot be left to craft alone. I thrive on rooting my point of view in this broader context and use it to help teams make good decisions. I don't shy away from complexity. Rather I use my intuition to lean into complex areas and then help frame problems in the right way for teams to deliver their best work.

Cheers!
