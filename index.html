WHOOP

Mobile & Web App Design

The WHOOP platform is designed to help elite athletes measure sleep quality, cardiovacular recovery, and training intensity. The platform includes a heart rate monitor working 24/7, mobile applications (iOS & Andoird), and a responsive web application.

WHOOP helps athletes from NFL running backs to marathon runners take the guess work out of timing fitness peaks and gives them an actionable tool to perform at a higher level.

Oomf

Mobile App Design

I designed the intial concepts and product design for the Oomf mobile app. Oomf is a smart charging service which allows users to rent/purchase chargers via their mobile device. I worked with stakeholders to define the MVP, strategies to launch the product, and the design of the app itself. The main goal of the app is to allow users to purchase charging time and unlock the charging capabilities of an Oomf charger through a unique code.

Check out TheOomf.com
